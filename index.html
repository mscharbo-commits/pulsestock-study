Critic AI — the most important tab for improvement. Two levels of analysis: (1) Postmortems — for every closed trade, the Critic asks why it won or lost, whether the stock selection was right or wrong, whether timing failed, whether the regime was wrong, and what the AI should learn. (2) Mistake Clusters — after 10+ closed trades, patterns emerge (e.g. "Breakouts fail 37% more often when VIX > 25"). These clusters become hard rules that are automatically injected into future Portfolio B picks.
